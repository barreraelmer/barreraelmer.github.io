Workday

Modernizing enterprise software to deliver better experiences for people at work.
As an intern on the Payroll, Time Tracking and Absence team, I was tasked with designing for the mobile time off experience.

Team

Sahar Salek, Product Manager

PROBLEM

A disjointed ecosystem

Past research showed that people needed to gather information from multiple places before they could confidently request time off from work. However, time off on Workday provided minimal utility.

OPPORTUNITY

Surface data to help people make better informed decisions

Leveraging past research surfaced the problem. In addition, I conducted product audits, competitive analyses, stakeholder and customer interview to further understand the nature of taking time off from work. Key insights from this research showed –

  1. New hires were confused about how different PTO plans differed
  2. People that work in collaborative environments value knowing when their teammates will be out of the office.
  3. People take work deadlines and company events into consideration when planning time off.
