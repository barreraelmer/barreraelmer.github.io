Matchmaker

In the Summer of 2016, I collaborated with SAP designers on behalf of the UC San Diego Design Lab to conceptualize a platform that matches educators and classes to real world projects.

I conducted competitive analysis, made wireframes, and designed an interactive storyboard prototype to communicate the idea behind the Matchmaker.
